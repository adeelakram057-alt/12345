  Study in Europe on a fully funded scholarship. Applications are now open for the **NAWA Banach Scholarship 2026 in Poland**. This prestigious scholarship is offered by the Polish National Agency for Academic Exchange (NAWA) in cooperation with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Poland, providing international students the opportunity to pursue a **Master’s** (second-cycle) degree at public and private universities in Poland. Programs are available in English or Polish, and selected candidates will receive full financial support. Studies are expected to begin in October 2026. 

  ### **Financial Coverage**

  The scholarship covers:

  - Full tuition fees
  - Monthly stipend
  - Living allowance
  - Possible relocation support
  - Access to top universities in Poland
  - **Fields of Study**
  - Engineering
  - Science
  - Arts
  - Social science
  - Technical
  - Sciences
  - Agricultural sciences
  - Life sciences.

  ### **Language of Study**

  - Programs are offered in:
      - English
      - Polish (with 1-year preparatory course if needed)

  ### **Eligibility Criteria**

  Applicants must meet the following requirements:

  - Be a citizen of a country listed below.
  - Polish citizens can not apply.
  - Not have previously obtained a master's degree.
  - Should have a bachelor’s degree.
  - Have not previously been granted a scholarship under the program.
  - Speak English at least at the B2 level.

  ### **Eligible Countries**

  The 2026 scholarship offer is open to citizens of the following countries: Albania, Angola, Argentina, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Brazil, Montenegro, the Philippines, Georgia, India, Indonesia, Iraq, Iran, Jordan, Kazakhstan, Kenya, Kosovo, Lebanon, North Macedonia, Mexico, Moldova, Mongolia, Nigeria, Palestine, Papua New Guinea, Peru, Rwanda, Senegal, Serbia, Tanzania, Tunisia, Ukraine, Uzbekistan, and Vietnam.
